About

I am a research-oriented Teaching Assistant at the Department of Electrical and Information Engineering, University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka. My work sits at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity, investigating how intelligent systems can be made more secure, and how security primitives can be enhanced through machine learning.

I am passionate about advancing knowledge through rigorous experimentation, analysis, and academic collaboration.

Research Interests

Artificial Intelligence

Machine learning models, predictive intelligence, and lightweight ML systems for real-time edge execution.

Cybersecurity & Defenses

Proactive threat detection, cryptographic integrity verification, and robust digital watermarking protocols.
